Accommodation Sector

Overview

The accommodation sector includes businesses that provide lodging services, such as hotels, motels, and resorts. This sector plays a crucial role in the tourism industry by offering a place for travelers to stay.

Industry Statistics

In 2019, the accommodation and food services sector represented 2.4% of New Brunswick’s GDP, contributing $0.7 billion. This sector employed 21,900 people, showing a 0.5% increase from 2010.
